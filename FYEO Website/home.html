Audit Process

What sets FYEO apart from the rest is not only our talent but our approach. We aim to give your team real-time updates throughout our line-by-line interactive auditing process.

1.Contact

Request an audit with details about your team and project and get MNDA in place.

2.Scheduling

We will schedule a review based on your availaility and your project's roadmap.

3.Scope

We scope your repository and provide you with a quote detailing estimated workload, timeline and pricing.

4.Audit

Our specialised review team will conduct our extensive auditing process, sharing any questions or findings as they arise with the development team.

5.Report

Deliver Draft Audit Report which will detail all of our findings and recommended fixes.

6.Remediation period

Clients will enter a 7 day period to implement and discuss our recommended fixes.

7.Re-Review

Our review team will re-review the implemented fixes to all findings documented in the report.

8.Final report

The review team sends an updated audit report that details the status of all findings highlighted in the report, confirming remediations or acknowledgement of all issues.
